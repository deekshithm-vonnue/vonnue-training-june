Vegetable Pasta - One Pot

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 zucchinis
    • Optional substitute: yellow squash
  • 1 red capsicum
    • Optional substitute: bell pepper
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 broccoli, cut into florets
  • 250g penne
  • 1.5 cups vegetable or chicken borth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Italian herb mix
  • 1 tsp garlic poweder
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot and cook garlic and onion until soft.
  2. Add zucchini and capsicum and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add crushed tomato, broth, tomato paste, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. When the mixture begins to boil, add pasta and broccoli.
  5. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Remove the lid and cook for another 4 minutes, stirring frequently.
  7. Add cheese if desired and cook until melted.
  8. Serve hot with parsley or parmesan.

Cooking Terms Glossary

Mince
To cut food into very small pieces.
chop
cut into pieces with repeated sharp blows of an axe or knife.
floret
one of the small flowers making up a composite flower head.
